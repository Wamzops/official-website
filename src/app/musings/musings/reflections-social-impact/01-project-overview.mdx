# Project Overview

I had never really thought about or acknowledged my privilege of access to food until I was selected as one of the seven volunteers from IEEE Kenyatta University to join the Uvumbuzi Tech Challenge. 

The challenge was part of InZone, a research and project centre operating under the auspices of the University of Geneva. I joined the Kakuma and Kalobeyei team as a trainer, where I contributed to the design and implementation of an engineering curriculum that was centered on the learners’ lived experiences.
